Organization

I organized several editions of the Brazilian Conference on Data Journalism and Digital Methods (Coda.Br) from 2018 to 2022, including the first regional meeting in the Amazon region. Previously, I collaborated with Witness to organize the first national meeting on deepfakes and developed a series of events about free culture and piracy (Copyfight).
